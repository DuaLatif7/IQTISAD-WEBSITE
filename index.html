About Us

Iqtisad-O-Elam is an unregistered nonprofit organization established in 2022.

  • We believe financial knowledge empowers individuals to shape their own futures.
  • We believe in making financial education accessible to all, fostering economic empowerment.
  • We believe that teaching financial literacy early builds a foundation for lifelong financial well-being.

Financial literacy is a critical subject that lays the foundation for sound financial decision-making and economic well-being. Understanding financial principles such as budgeting, saving, investing, and managing debt equips individuals with the knowledge to navigate complex financial landscapes.
